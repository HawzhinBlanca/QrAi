Some information to know before deciding  :
On-demand: With on-demand pricing, you pay for the 
resources that you use, and you can scale your server 
up or down as needed.
Reserved: With reserved pricing, you commit to using 
a certain amount of resources for a certain amount of 
time, and you receive a discount on the price of those 
resources.
Spot: With spot pricing, you can bid on unused AWS 
resources, and you only pay for the resources that you 
use. However, there is no guarantee that you will be 
able to get the resources that you need, and the price 
of spot resources can ﬂuctuate wildly.
